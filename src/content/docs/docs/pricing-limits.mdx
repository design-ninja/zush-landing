Zush has a free starter tier for testing the workflow and paid PRO plans for ongoing use. Check the [pricing section](/#pricing) for the current checkout options before purchasing.

## Free tier

The free tier gives you 50 AI renames to test Zush on real files. [Install Zush](/docs/install-update) and use the free renames on a small batch that represents your normal work: screenshots, PDFs, photos, documents, design files, audio, or video.

Templates, Naming Blocks, [Custom AI Blocks](/docs/custom-ai-blocks), and custom prompts are included free — you can build your full naming setup before upgrading.

## PRO

PRO is built for ongoing file organization. Current public product copy describes two PRO options:

| Plan | Fit |
| --- | --- |
| Monthly PRO | Flexible access for ongoing AI file renaming. |
| One-Time PRO | One-time purchase for users who prefer lifetime access. |

PRO includes unlimited PRO renames and unlocks BYOK and Offline AI. Templates, Naming Blocks, Custom AI Blocks, and custom prompts are available on the free tier as well.

To upgrade, open the current [pricing section](/#pricing) and choose Monthly PRO or One-Time PRO.

## BYOK costs

BYOK lets you use your own Gemini, Groq, OpenAI, or Claude provider key. Start with the [BYOK setup guide](/docs/byok). Zush does not set your provider's API pricing; your provider bills usage according to its own terms.

## Offline AI costs

Offline AI uses local Ollama models. Start with the [Offline AI guide](/docs/offline-ai) or download [Ollama](https://ollama.com/download). Zush does not charge per local model call, but local processing depends on your computer, installed models, disk space, and model performance.
